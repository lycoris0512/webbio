Biography

Hello My Friends. Welcome!

Hello, my name is Nguyen Truong Cuong. I am currently a Pharmacy student and a freelance developer with a passion for building polished digital experiences that bridge the gap between healthcare and technology.

While technology and software development are strong passions of mine, my academic heart lies in the medical and biological sciences. I spend a lot of time diving deep into human anatomy, histology, and the intricate world of biochemistry and organic chemistry. During my free time, I enjoy exploring creative coding projects and discovering how technical solutions can be applied to scientific fields, from qualitative analysis to understanding complex biological structures like lipid membranes.

Outside of the laboratory and coding, music, gaming, and spending time with friends are some of my favorite activities. Valorant is one of the games I enjoy the most because it sharpens my reflexes, strategic thinking, and teamwork under pressure.

With a relentless drive for continuous learning in both pharmacy and tech, my goal is to become a versatile professional capable of building innovative, tech-driven medical solutions in the future.
